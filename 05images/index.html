Featured Games of the week!

Minecraft

  1. Minecraft is a game all about exploring a random generated blocky world full of different biomes and structures. Players can break the blocks that the world is made out of to dig holes or replace those blocks somewhere else. There is also crafting to make different tools and weapons, and a day and night cycle featuring monsters that spawn during the night and serve as a threat to players who stay outside of shelter.

Roblox

  1. Roblox is a big website, free to play, with millions of users worldwide. It is a MMO designed for all ages allowing its users to play games created on the website by other players. Inside these games users control a blocky character called a avatar, that can be customized with accessories, clothing, different skin color, and other items that the user owns in their account. Roblox has a currency called Robux that has to be bought with real money, that can be used to buy accessories that cost robux in the catalog or gamepasses that give owners different items or powerups inside games.

What are Video games?

Pretty sure everyone knows this but if not, video games are a form of entertainment that involves a program that lets you control a character in another world, like in RPGs they let you experience being a knight in a medieval world taking on huge monsters and beasts. Also video games can be a way to socialize with other people or friends through the internet.
