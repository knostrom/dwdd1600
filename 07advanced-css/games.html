Roblox

  • Roblox is a big website, free to play, with millions of users worldwide. It is a MMO designed for all ages allowing its users to play games created on the website by other players. Inside these games users control a blocky character called a avatar, that can be customized with accessories, clothing, different skin color, and other items that the user owns in their account. Roblox has a currency called Robux that has to be bought with real money, that can be used to buy accessories that cost robux in the catalog or gamepasses that give owners different items or powerups inside games.
  • Current price: Free!!!
  • Sign up Here

World of Warcraft

  • World of Warcraft or WoW is an online MMO where you play with friends to explore a massive world and fight dangerous creatures.
  • Current Price: $29.99

Minecraft

  • Minecraft is a game all about exploring a random generated blocky world full of different biomes and structures. Players can break the blocks the world is made out of to dig holes or replace those blocks somewhere else, there is also crafting to make different tools and weapons, and a day and night cycle featuring monsters that spawn during the night and serve as a threat to players who stay outside of shelter.
  • Current Price: $29.99, Deluxe Price: $39.99
  • Buy it Here

Lego Fortnite

  • This game is alot like Minecraft but you're surviving as Fortnite characters that have been trapped in a random generated world of lego and has its own new mechanics and features.
  • Current price: Free!!!

Subnautica

  • A survival game taking place in the ocean stranded on a alien planet where you must build things utilizing the planets resources to survive and eventually escape the planet and get back home.
  • Current Price: $29.99
